The Treatment Process

The process of laser hair removal involves using a handheld device that emits concentrated light beams to target hair follicles. Clients might feel a slight tingling or snapping sensation during the procedure, which most find tolerable. Each session can last between 15 to 60 minutes, depending on the treatment area size. Multiple sessions are necessary to achieve the desired results, as hair grows in cycles, and the laser targets follicles in the active growth phase.

Post-Treatment Care

Following laser hair removal Mississauga, it’s vital to adhere to post-treatment care instructions for optimal results. This includes avoiding direct sun exposure, applying soothing creams, and refraining from using harsh skin products on the treated area. Clients typically notice a significant reduction in hair growth after a few sessions, with the effects becoming more pronounced with continued treatment.
